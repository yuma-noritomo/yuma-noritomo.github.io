Yuma Noritomo

I'm Yuma, a PhD student at Dyson, Cornell. I visited PSE in Fall 2024, with shorter research visits at Utrecht and Wageningen. Reach out to me at yn266[at]cornell.edu

My research focuses on strategies to reduce chronic poverty through human capital accumulation in low-income, challenging settings.
